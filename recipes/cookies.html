Best Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

This recipe is for the perfect chocolate chip cookie. It delivers a cookie that is satisfyingly crispy on the edges but wonderfully soft and chewy in the center. They are the ultimate comfort food.

The secret to their texture is using melted butter and an extra egg yolk, which ensures they stay moist. These cookies are dangerously easy to make and even harder to resist.

Ingredients:

  • Dry Ingredients:
    • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1/2 tsp salt

  • Wet Ingredients:
    • 3/4 cup (170g) unsalted butter, melted
    • 1 cup packed brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 large egg + 1 extra egg yolk
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract

  • The Best Part:
    • 2 cups (340g) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C (375°F). Line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until well combined.

  4. Add Eggs: Beat in the egg, the extra egg yolk, and the vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth.

  5. Combine: Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredient mixture. Mix with a spatula until just combined. Do not overmix the dough.

  6. Add Chocolate: Gently fold in the chocolate chips.

  7. Scoop: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving about 2 inches of space between them.

  8. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are still slightly soft.

  9. Cool: Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
