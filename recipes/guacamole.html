Classic Guacamole Recipe

Description:

Guacamole is a classic Mexican dip made primarily from avocados. It's refreshing, creamy, and packed with flavor. This recipe keeps it simple and authentic, focusing on the fresh taste of the avocado, which is perfectly balanced with sharp red onion, spicy jalapeño, and fresh cilantro.

It's the perfect party snack to serve with tortilla chips or as a fresh topping for tacos and burritos. The key is using ripe avocados and mashing them by hand to maintain a chunky texture.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 jalapeños, seeds removed and minced

  • For the Flavour Base:
    • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro (coriander), chopped
    • Juice of 1-2 large limes
    • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
    • 1/4 tsp cumin (optional, for a smoky flavor)

  • Optional Add-ins:
    • 2 Roma tomatoes, deseeded and diced
    • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Avocados: Halve the avocados, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a medium-sized bowl.

  2. Mash: Using a fork or a potato masher, gently mash the avocados to your desired consistency. We recommend keeping it slightly chunky.

  3. Add Ingredients: Add the chopped red onion, minced jalapeño, and chopped cilantro to the bowl.

  4. Season: Squeeze the juice of one lime over the mixture. Add the salt and cumin (if using).

  5. Mix: Gently fold all the ingredients together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix!

  6. Taste and Adjust: Taste the guacamole. Add more lime juice for tanginess or more salt if needed. If using tomatoes or garlic, fold them in now.

  7. Serve: Serve immediately with your favorite tortilla chips or as a topping.
